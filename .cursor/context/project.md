Project Name: Motor Auto Care CRM

This is a modern AI-powered workshop management system.

Target users:
- Automobile workshop owners
- Mechanics
- Managers

Main capabilities:

- Job card management
- Vehicle service history
- Inventory tracking
- Staff salary management
- Appointment scheduling
- Cashbook and expenses
- Reports and analytics
- AI assistant for business insights

The UI must be mobile-first and resemble workshop dashboard apps.

The system must scale to multi-garage SaaS in future.
